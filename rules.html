Rules

Sprinkler & Irrigation Lines
We must be informed of sprinkler lines as we do use heavy duty steel stakes to hold down our equipment. We are not responsible for any irrigation line damage.
High Winds
Blower must be turned off immediately if winds exceed 25 mph. Our equipment is not safe in excessive wind no matter how it is being held down. The inflatable will deflate as soon as the blower is powered down.
Before Entering
Everyone using the inflatable unit must remove shoes, jewelry, and items that may be in pockets before entering the inflatable.
Netting
No climbing, putting fingers through the netting or pulling on the netting.
Food
No food, gum, candy or silly string allowed inside the inflatable.
